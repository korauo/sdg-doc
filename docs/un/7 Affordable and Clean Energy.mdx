# 7. Affordable and Clean Energy

<p align="center"><img src="https://www.un.org/sustainabledevelopment/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/E-Goal-07-1024x1024.png" width="200" height="200"/></p>

## **Objective**

**SDG 7 focuses on ensuring access to affordable, reliable, sustainable, and modern energy for all. It recognizes that access to clean energy is crucial for eradicating poverty, improving education and healthcare, and promoting economic growth. This goal emphasizes the transition towards renewable energy sources, energy efficiency, and expanding energy infrastructure in developing countries.**

## Targets:
    
### 7.1. Ensure universal access to affordable, reliable, and modern energy services.

### 7.2. Increase the share of renewable energy in the global energy mix and promote energy efficiency in all sectors.

### 7.3. Enhance international cooperation to support developing countries in accessing clean energy technologies and expanding their energy infrastructure.

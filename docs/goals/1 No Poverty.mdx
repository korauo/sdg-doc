# 1. No Poverty
<p align="center"><img src="https://un.org/sustainabledevelopment/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/E-Goal-01-1024x1024.png" width="200" height="200"/></p>

## **Objective** 
**SDG 1 strives to end poverty in all its forms and dimensions. It recognizes that poverty is not solely characterized by lack of income but encompasses multiple dimensions, such as limited access to education, healthcare, clean water, and sanitation. This goal aims to ensure that every individual has equal opportunities to thrive and live a life free from poverty and deprivation.**

## Targets

### 1.1. By 2030, eradicate extreme poverty for all people living below the international poverty line.

### 1.2. Implement social protection systems to ensure adequate support for those in need.

### 1.3. Implement sustainable poverty reduction strategies.

# 3. Good Health and Well-being
<p align="center"><img src="https://www.un.org/sustainabledevelopment/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/E-Goal-03-1024x1024.png" width="200" height="200"/></p>

## **Objective**

**SDG 3 aims to ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for all at all ages. It recognizes that health is a fundamental human right and a key enabler of sustainable development. This goal addresses a broad spectrum of health issues, ranging from maternal and child health to communicable and non-communicable diseases.**

## Targets:

### 3.1. Reduce maternal mortality and end preventable deaths of newborns and children under five.

### 3.2. Prevent and treat communicable diseases, including HIV/AIDS, malaria, and tuberculosis.

### 3.3. Strengthen the prevention and treatment of mental health disorders.

# 6. Clean Water and Sanitation

<p align="center"><img src="https://www.un.org/sustainabledevelopment/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/E-Goal-06-1024x1024.png" width="200" height="200"/></p>

## **Objective**

** SDG 6 aims to ensure availability and sustainable management of water and sanitation for all. It recognizes the essential role of clean water and sanitation in eradicating poverty, promoting health, and preserving ecosystems. This goal addresses water scarcity, water pollution, and inadequate sanitation facilities, with a focus on providing access to safe and affordable drinking water and sanitation services.**

## Targets:
    
### 6.1. Achieve universal and equitable access to safe and affordable drinking water for all.

### 6.2. Achieve access to adequate and equitable sanitation and hygiene for all.

### 6.3. Improve water quality and reduce pollution, wastewater generation, and water scarcity.

# 2. Zero Hunger

<p align="center"><img src="https://www.un.org/sustainabledevelopment/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/E-Goal-02-1024x1024.png" width="200" height="200"/></p>

## **Objective**

**SDG 2 focuses on achieving food security, improving nutrition, and promoting sustainable agriculture. It recognizes the critical importance of ending hunger and ensuring access to nutritious and sufficient food for all. This goal emphasizes the need for sustainable agricultural practices, investment in rural infrastructure, and the reduction of food waste along the entire food value chain.**

## Targets

### 2.1. End hunger and ensure access to safe, nutritious, and sufficient food for all people

### 2.2. End all forms of malnutrition, including stunting and wasting in children

### 2.3. Double agricultural productivity and incomes of small-scale food producers

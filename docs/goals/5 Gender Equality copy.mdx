# 5. Gender Equality

<p align="center"><img src="https://www.un.org/sustainabledevelopment/wp-content/uploads/2019/08/E-Goal-05-1024x1024.png" width="200" height="200"/></p>

## **Objective**

**SDG 5 aims to achieve gender equality and empower all women and girls. It recognizes that gender equality is not only a fundamental human right but also a necessary foundation for a peaceful, prosperous, and sustainable world. This goal addresses gender-based discrimination and violence while promoting equal access to education, economic opportunities, and leadership positions.**

## Targets:
    
### 5.1. End all forms of discrimination and violence against women and girls.

### 5.2. Eliminate harmful practices such as child marriage and female genital mutilation.

### 5.3. Ensure women's full and effective participation in political, economic, and public life.
